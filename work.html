Basecamp

The entire production process is tied together by the Basecamp Project collaboration service. The service allows clients and production staff to keep up to date and involved at every stage of the work.

Determine the need and define functionality and featureset.

Our design staff will lay out the user interface and generate the creative content. It is then submitted for revision and approval by the client.

Our engineers implement the design and test for functionality and stability. Multiple milestone builds may be submitted for client testing depending on the scope of the project.

Delivery of source code/built application is made, or submission to the app store or market is performed.
